# Raise The Bar (RTB)

**Author:** Ramdas  
**Source:** LinkedIn Blog

---

## Introduction

The "Raise The Bar (RTB)" initiative began during the COVID-19 pandemic in March 2020.  
The goal of this initiative was to reimagine and rebuild the technology platform in order to deliver better outcomes for clients while also improving the internal engineering standards of the team.

---

## Platform Architecture

The RTB platform is designed using modern software architecture principles such as:

- NoCode constructs
- Domain-Driven Design (DDD)
- Microservices architecture

These technologies help in building scalable, flexible, and maintainable systems.

---

## Platform Offerings

Using the RTB platform, two major platform solutions were developed:

1. **IncrediHub**
2. **IncentiHub**

These platforms support solutions in areas such as:

- Lending Technology (LendingTech)
- Channel Technology (ChannelTech)

---

## HeartBeat Portal

To manage growing infrastructure complexity, a lightweight monitoring system called **HeartBeat Portal** was developed.

This portal helps monitor **AWS ECS clusters across multiple environments**, improving operational visibility and system reliability.
