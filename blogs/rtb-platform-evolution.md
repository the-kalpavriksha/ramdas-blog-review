# The Evolution of RTB – Real Time Business Services

**Author:** Ramdas  
**Source:** LinkedIn Post  

---

## Introduction

This post shares the backstory of the **RTB (Real Time Business Services) platform**, a platform developed at WonderLend Hubs over the last several years.

The journey began in March 2020 with an internal mission called **Mission RTB – Raise The Bar**.  
The idea was inspired by the mindset of constantly pushing boundaries and improving outcomes for customers.

---

## The Motivation Behind RTB

The starting question was simple:

Why do complex financial technology platforms have to be **rigid, brittle systems with artificial boundaries**?

Financial systems often struggle to adapt to continuous change. Instead of fighting change, the team chose to **embrace change as a core design principle**.

This philosophy aligns with the famous idea from the Greek philosopher **Heraclitus**:

> Change is the only constant.

---

## Platform Design Philosophy

The RTB platform is designed using modern architecture principles:

- **NoCode constructs**
- **Domain-Driven Design (DDD)**
- **Microservices architecture**

These technologies allow complex financial systems to remain **flexible, modular, and adaptable**.

---

## The Symphony Metaphor

The author compares modern platforms to a **symphony orchestra**.

Just like an orchestra:

- Different instruments play different roles
- The conductor ensures harmony
- The performance evolves dynamically

Similarly, modern platforms orchestrate:

- Services
- APIs
- Data flows
- Business policies

The system becomes less like a rigid machine and more like a **coordinated performance**.

---

## Inspiration from Art and Performance

Two artistic inspirations are mentioned:

- **Beethoven's Emperor Concerto conducted by Zubin Mehta**
- **The Shiv Tandav Ballet with music by Shankar Mahadevan**

These performances symbolize the idea of **complex components working together to create something powerful and elegant**.

---

## The Power of “Why”

The author strongly believes in the **Power of Why**.

While open-source communities often share the **“How”** of technology, there is much less discussion about the **“Why” behind architectural ideas**.

This post attempts to **open-source the thinking behind the RTB platform**.

---

## Internal Platform Milestone

The internal MVP of the RTB platform was launched within WonderLend Hubs in **July 2022**.

The journey involved significant experimentation, collaboration, and innovation from the product and engineering teams.
