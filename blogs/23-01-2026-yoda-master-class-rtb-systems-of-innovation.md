# Yoda Master Class Week #1 – Systems of Innovation and the RTB Platform

**Author:** Ram Ramdas  
**Source:** LinkedIn Post  

---

## Overview

In this LinkedIn post, Ram Ramdas discusses how **Systems of Innovation** require a fundamentally different mindset compared to traditional enterprise systems.

He argues that organizations cannot design innovation systems using the same thinking used for **Systems of Record** or **Systems of Process**.

The post also shares the inspiration behind the **RTB – Real Time Business Services platform** developed at WonderLend Hubs.

---

## Systems of Innovation

Traditional enterprise software typically falls into two categories:

### Systems of Record
These include:

- loan management systems  
- accounting systems  
- policy administration systems  

They manage core data and transactions.

### Systems of Process
These include:

- workflow systems  
- CRM platforms  
- business process automation  

They coordinate operational processes across teams.

However, Ram Ramdas argues that **Systems of Innovation must go beyond these categories** and become central to how organizations evolve their business models.

---

## Inspiration from Art and Performance

The evolution of the RTB platform was inspired by artistic metaphors.

Examples include:

- **Shiv Tandav ballet** with music by Shankar Mahadevan
- **Beethoven’s “Emperor” symphony** conducted by Zubin Mehta

These performances represent how complex systems can produce powerful outcomes when many components work together in harmony.

The same metaphor applies to modern financial technology platforms.

---

## The Power of “Why”

The author emphasizes that the technology community often focuses heavily on **open sourcing the “How”** of systems.

However, he believes it is equally important to open source the **“Why”** — the underlying inspirations and conceptual thinking behind technology platforms.

This post shares the internal presentation used by the team to communicate the **vision and philosophy behind RTB**.

---

## RTB – Real Time Business Services

The RTB platform aims to support the development of **modern fintech innovation systems**.

Key technologies associated with the platform include:

- NoCode architectures
- Domain Driven Design (DDD)
- Microservices
- Serverless infrastructure

The goal is to build flexible platforms capable of supporting rapidly evolving financial services ecosystems.

## Conclusion

The post invites readers to explore the deeper inspirations behind the RTB platform and how these ideas might help them rethink the design of innovation systems.
